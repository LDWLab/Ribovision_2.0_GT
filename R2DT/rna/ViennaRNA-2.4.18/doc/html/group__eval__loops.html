Detailed Description

Functions to evaluate the free energy of particular types of loops.

To assess the free energy contribution of a particular loop within a secondary structure, two variants are provided:

  • The bare free energy (usually in deka-calories, i.e. multiples of ), and
  • The Boltzmann weight of the free energy (with , gas constant and temperature )

The latter is usually required for partition function computations.
